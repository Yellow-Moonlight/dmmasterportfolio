Seongjoo Moon

On My Head

Participants will attend a workshop where they purchase materials from Dongdaemun Garment Material Market to decorate hats based on a chosen theme. They will select materials within a 10,000won(ca. 7€) budget, decorate their hats, and then discuss their creations.

2022

Participants : Gijin Park, In Hong, Yunseok Jeong, Seunghee Lee, Kayun Kim, Taeyun Jeon, Bokyeong Yun, Yuntae Kim
